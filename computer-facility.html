With high-speed internet connectivity, modern hardware, and a wide range of software applications, our computer facility provides the perfect environment for learning and growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our computer facility and courses

Do I need prior computer experience to join your courses?
No, we offer courses for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced users. Our instructors will assess your current level and recommend the appropriate course for you.
What are the timings for the computer facility?
Our computer facility is open from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. We offer flexible batch timings, including morning, afternoon, and evening sessions to accommodate different schedules.
Do you provide certificates upon course completion?
Yes, we provide industry-recognized certificates upon successful completion of our courses. These certificates can help you showcase your skills to potential employers.
What is the student-to-instructor ratio in your classes?
We maintain a low student-to-instructor ratio of 10:1 to ensure personalized attention and effective learning. This allows our instructors to address individual learning needs effectively.
Can I access the computer facility outside of class hours?
Yes, enrolled students can access the computer facility during designated practice hours, subject to availability. We encourage students to practice and reinforce their learning outside of regular class sessions.
