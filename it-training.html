Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our IT training programs

Do I need prior programming experience to join your courses?
No, we offer courses for all skill levels. Our beginner courses start from the basics and assume no prior programming knowledge. For intermediate and advanced courses, we recommend completing the prerequisite courses or having equivalent experience.
What kind of projects will I work on during the course?
You'll work on real-world projects that simulate actual industry scenarios. These include web applications, mobile apps, data analysis projects, and more. Each project is designed to reinforce the concepts learned and build your portfolio.
Are the instructors industry professionals?
Yes, all our instructors are experienced IT professionals with years of industry experience. They bring practical knowledge and current industry insights to the classroom, ensuring you learn skills that are relevant in today's job market.
Do you provide job placement assistance?
Yes, we provide comprehensive job placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and connecting you with our network of hiring partners. Many of our students secure job offers even before completing their courses.
Can I take multiple courses simultaneously?
While it's possible to take multiple courses, we recommend focusing on one course at a time to ensure you can fully absorb the material and complete projects effectively. Our advisors can help you create a learning path that suits your goals and schedule.
