Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our English speaking courses

Do I need prior knowledge of English to join your courses?
No prior knowledge is required for our beginner courses. We offer programs for all proficiency levels, from absolute beginners to advanced speakers. Our instructors will assess your current level and place you in the appropriate course.
How long does it take to become fluent in English?
The time to fluency varies depending on your starting level, learning pace, and practice outside class. Generally, students can achieve conversational fluency in 6-12 months with consistent effort. Our courses are designed to accelerate your learning through structured practice.
What teaching methods do you use in your English courses?
We use a communicative approach that emphasizes practical usage over rote memorization. Our methods include interactive conversations, role-plays, group discussions, multimedia resources, and real-life scenarios to make learning engaging and effective.
Do you provide study materials for the courses?
Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials including textbooks, workbooks, audio resources, and access to our online learning platform. All materials are carefully selected to complement our teaching methodology and enhance your learning experience.
Can I switch between different learning modes if needed?
Yes, we offer flexibility in learning modes. If your circumstances change, you can discuss switching between classroom, online, or hybrid learning with our course coordinators. We'll help you find the best option to continue your learning journey without interruption.
